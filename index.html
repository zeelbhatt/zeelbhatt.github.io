02 — About

Teaching machines to understand shapes

I'm an M.S. Robotics graduate from Arizona State University working in machine learning, with a focus on 3D vision and robotics.

My research is driven by a more abstract interest in shape understanding — at both the structural and semantic levels. These days I work in the construction industry, building 3D technology for the field.  Outside of work, I play a lot of chess and a musical instrument — and I believe humans should be immortal.
